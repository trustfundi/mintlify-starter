---

## Respect and safety

Use Lumira and Lumira community spaces respectfully. Harassment, hate speech,
threats, impersonation, abuse, raids, or attempts to harm communities are not
allowed.

## Platform rules

You must follow Discord's terms and Roblox's terms while using Lumira.

## Service misuse

Do not use Lumira to:

- Abuse ranking permissions
- Mass-demote or raid communities
- Evade bans or moderation actions
- Mislead staff or support
- Share harmful content
- Expose private information

## Enforcement

Lumira may restrict, suspend or remove access if a user or workspace misuses the
service.
