## **1. Discord Terms & Community Standards**

All members must follow the Discord Terms of Service and Community Guidelines while participating in the Lumira Discord server. Failure to comply may result in moderation actions, including removal from the community.

### 2. Respectful Conduct

We expect all members to treat others with respect. Harassment, unnecessary drama, or behaviour intended to disrupt the community may result in warnings or removal at the discretion of the moderation team. Lumira aims to maintain a welcoming and positive environment for everyone.

### 3. Harassment & Discrimination

Bullying, harassment, or discrimination based on race, gender, religion, nationality, identity, or other personal characteristics is not permitted. Violations may result in moderation actions, including bans from the server.

### 4. Inappropriate Content

Sharing explicit, offensive, or otherwise inappropriate material—including NSFW content, hate speech, or abusive language—is not allowed within **Lumira** community spaces.

### 5. Spam & Disruptive Behaviour

Spam, mass messaging, excessive emoji use, or attempts to disrupt the community experience are not permitted. Repeated violations may lead to temporary or **permanent** restrictions.

### 6. Advertising & Promotions

Advertising external services, communities, or websites without prior approval from Lumira staff is not allowed. Unsolicited direct messages to members for promotional purposes may also lead to moderation actions.

### 7. Privacy & Confidential Information

Please respect the privacy of others. Do not share personal information, private conversations, or internal staff discussions without permission.

### 8. Community Conduct

Members should contribute positively to the community. behaviour intended to disrupt, damage, or undermine the community environment may result in moderation action.

### 9. Fraudulent or Misleading Activity

Any attempts to mislead others, impersonate individuals, or exploit **Lumira** services may lead to removal from the platform.

### 10. Illegal or Harmful Activities

Using Lumira services to promote or participate in illegal or harmful activities is not permitted and may result in removal from the community.

### 11. Support System Usage

Please use **Lumira’s** support systems responsibly. Abusive or disruptive use of support channels may result in restricted access.

### 12. Intellectual Property

Respect the intellectual property rights of others. Do not share or distribute copyrighted material without proper permission.

### Moderation & Enforcement

**Lumira moderators** reserve the right to take appropriate action when guidelines are violated. This may include warnings, temporary restrictions, or **permanent** removal depending on the **severity** of the **situation**.
