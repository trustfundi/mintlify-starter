## Verified users page

The verified users page shows Discord members and whether they have linked a
Roblox account through Lumira.

## Status tags

- **Verified** means Lumira has a saved Discord-to-Roblox link.
- **Unverified** means the member has not completed verification.

## Member profiles

Click a member to view their profile, moderation history, summary, and logs where
data is available.
