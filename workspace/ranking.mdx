---

## Ranking actions

Lumira supports:

- Promote
- Demote
- Set rank

All rank actions must go through Lumira's ranking service and the connected
Roblox OAuth ranking account.

## Requirements

- Ranking account linked
- Ranking enabled in workspace settings
- Target user is in the Roblox group
- Ranking account can manage the target rank

## Logs

Ranking actions are logged with the actor, target, old rank, new rank, action,
reason, and timestamp where available.

<Warning>
  Lumira cannot rank users above the connected ranking account's Roblox group
  permissions.
</Warning>
