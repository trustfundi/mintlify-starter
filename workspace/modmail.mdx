---
title: "Modmail"
description: "Open private support tickets between users and staff."
---

## What modmail does

Modmail lets users contact a community privately. Lumira creates a ticket channel
for staff and relays messages between the user and the ticket.

## Staff access

Configure one or more staff roles that can access tickets.

## Claiming tickets

Staff can claim a ticket to show ownership. The assigned staff member is stored
on the modmail thread and appears on dashboard transcript views.

## Transcripts

When a ticket closes, Lumira can post or store a transcript. Transcript URLs use:

```text
{domain}/{guildId}/tickets/{ticketReference}
```
