## Forms builder

Forms let workspace owners collect structured responses from members.

Supported field types include:

- Short text
- Long text
- Multiple choice
- Dropdown
- Checkbox
- Number
- File upload where enabled

## Responses

Responses are stored in the workspace and can be reviewed from the dashboard.
Configured forms can also post a formatted Discord embed.
