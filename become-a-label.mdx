**Lumira affiliates** with established and active communities that align with our **importance** and **morals**. Our **Label affiliates** program is designed to **support long-term collaboration.**

<Info>
  **All label applications are handled through our email service. This ensures a streamlined review process and direct communication with our Marketing & Public Relations Team.**
</Info>

---

### Label Requirements

- **100\+ non-botted Discord server members**.
- **50\+ non-botted Roblox community members**.
- The group must be an **active community**, with visible activity within the past **two weeks**.
- Must maintain a **clean, respectful community** free of toxicity or **ongoing** drama.
- Must have a **clearly defined leadership structure** and responsible **staff team**.

<Warning>
  Failure to continue holding these standards, can result in either our label agreement to be terminated or paused under investigation.
</Warning>

---

### Application Process

If you are still interested in applying for our label program, make sure to contact us via **partners@lumiraranking.co.uk**. Our response time will range, within 48 hours of application being sent. 

### Why join our Label Program?

Joining the **Lumira Label Program** offers communities the opportunity to grow, collaborate, and gain recognition within a supportive network. 

By becoming a **Label**, your **community receives**:\
• **Increased visibility** through cross-promotion and representation across Lumira platforms.\
• **Direct communication** with our Marketing & Public Relations Team for coordinated outreach and announcements.\
• **Long-term partnership benefits**, including priority consideration for events, collaborative projects, and promotional initiatives.\
• **Access to a trusted network** of vetted, active communities that uphold similar values and standards.\
• **Support and guidance** to help strengthen your community structure, engagement, and overall professionalism.

Our goal is to create meaningful, lasting partnerships and help your community reach its full potential while contributing to a positive, respectful, and active ecosystem.
