---
title: "Connect Discord"
description: "Invite Lumira and configure server-side Discord settings."
---

## Invite the bot

Use the invite button in onboarding or workspace settings. Lumira needs Discord
permissions to:

- Read roles and channels
- Post session, LOA, modmail, and workflow embeds
- Manage configured roles
- Send direct messages when possible
- Log configured workspace actions

## Configure channels

After the bot joins, set channels for:

- Session announcements
- Moderation logs
- LOA requests
- Modmail transcripts
- Verification instructions

## Configure access roles

Workspace access roles decide who can open the dashboard. Sensitive settings
should remain limited to the owner and trusted co-owners.
