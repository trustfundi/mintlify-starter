## Requirements

- A Discord account
- Access to the Discord server you want to manage
- Permission to invite the Lumira bot
- A Roblox group you are allowed to manage

## Create the workspace

1. Open the Lumira portal.
2. Sign in with Discord.
3. Select **New Workspace**.
4. Choose your Discord server.
5. Follow the setup flow until the bot invite step appears.

The creator is saved as the workspace owner. Other users with access roles can
view or manage non-sensitive areas depending on the workspace configuration.
